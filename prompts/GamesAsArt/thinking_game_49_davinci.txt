 Journey, by the independent games developer thatgamecompany. It is an adventure, in which the player controls a robed figure across a desert in a barren landscape. The player is not given a goal, but instead explores the world, learning about the history of the place and helping other players that they encounter. The game gives the player no instructions, but the player is able to figure out how to play by exploring the world and trying different actions. The game encourages the player to explore and discover the world on their own.
