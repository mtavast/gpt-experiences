 The Path (gameplay video), it's a very simple game. You are in the woods, you need to find The Mother, and to do that you need to go through the woods. There are paths, and the paths are blocked by barbed wire. You can jump over it or you can go around it. If you jump over it you get a point. And you get a point if you find The Mother. But you can't just find The Mother, you have to find a key in order to get in her house.
